Local trader

Local Trader is an Android application for LocalBitcoins. Use Local Trader to create and update advertisements, manage trades, download attachments, release and fund trades, contact advertisers, or to handle payments.

TRADE

With Local Tarder you can search for buyers or sellers, release and fund trades, receive trade messages or create and manage your own advertisements.

WALLET

Local Trader offers an optional feature to send money from your LocalBitcoins wallet (with proper permissions). You can scan or generate QR codes for payments or review wallet history.

SECURITY

Local Trader offers secure HMAC authentication that allows users to select the permissions they want. A PIN code is required to fund and release trades or to send money.

Fully Featured

Local Trader uses the LocalBitcoins service and and authentication to bring you a safe and secure way to mange your trades.
